How to Submit a Ticket

By Ian Mason


Step 1: Open an internet Browser

Open the browser of your choice. Our ticketing system supports all of the current mainline internet browsers such as Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Opera, and Safari

For this example I will be utilizing Firefox, but again please choose whichever browser you are most comfortbale with.

campaign If you experiencing a classroom emergency or need to speak someone urgently regarding your issue please call 877-382

Step 2: Navigate to the Support Webpage

In the navigation box of the browser enter support.kccd.edu

For this example I will be utilizing Firefox, but again please choose whichever browser you are most comfortbale with.

Step 3: Login

Creating a ticket within our support tracking system requires logging into your account.

Please click the login button on the top right to proceed.

Step 4: Click on "Create a Case"

The help articles are there for your guidance if you are having trouble with something you belieive you can troubleshoot on your own, and for known issues that may require a temporary work-around.

Password reset will take you to uam.kccd.edu which is the self help portal for resetting your password.

The last option is the button we want in this article, which is "Create a Case".

Step 5: Submitting the Form

Thbis can be the most daunting part of submitting a ticket. It may look like it is asking for lots of technical jargon that you may or may not be familiar with. What the IT team would like to see is the best description of the issue you are having in your own terms.

Along with describing the issue, any steps taking to try to resolve the problem before you came to create a ticket will be immensely helpful for the support team.
